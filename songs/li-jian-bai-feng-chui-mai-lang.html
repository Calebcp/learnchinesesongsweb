风吹麦浪 Fēng chuī mài làng
Wind Blows the Wheat Waves

李健 Li Jian

远处蔚蓝天空下
Yuǎn chù wèi lán tiān kōng xià
Far away beneath the azure sky
涌动着金色的麦浪
Yǒng dòng zhe jīn sè de mài làng
Golden wheat waves surge
就在那里曾是你和我
Jiù zài nà lǐ céng shì nǐ hé wǒ
Right there was where you and I once
爱过的地方
Ài guò de dì fang
Loved each other
当微风带着收获的味道
Dāng wēi fēng dài zhe shōu huò de wèi dào
When the gentle breeze carries the scent of harvest
吹向我脸庞
Chuī xiàng wǒ liǎn páng
Blowing across my face
想起你轻柔的话语
Xiǎng qǐ nǐ qīng róu de huà yǔ
I recall your soft words
曾打湿我眼眶
Céng dǎ shī wǒ yǎn kuàng
That once moistened my eyes
我们曾在田野里歌唱
Wǒ men céng zài tián yě lǐ gē chàng
We once sang in the fields
在冬季盼望
Zài dōng jì pàn wàng
Looking forward through winter
却没能等到阳光下
Què méi néng děng dào yáng guāng xià
Yet never lived to see
这秋天的景象
Zhè qiū tiān de jǐng xiàng
This autumn scenery under the sun
就让失散的誓言飞舞吧
Jiù ràng shī sàn de shì yán fēi wǔ ba
Let the scattered vows dance in the air
随西风飘荡
Suí xī fēng piāo dàng
Drifting with the west wind
就像你柔软的长发
Jiù xiàng nǐ róu ruǎn de cháng fā
Just like your soft long hair
曾芬芳我梦乡
Céng fēn fāng wǒ mèng xiāng
That once perfumed my dreams
