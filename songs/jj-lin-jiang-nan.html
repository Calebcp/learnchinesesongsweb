江南 Jiāng nán
Jiang Nan (River South)

林俊杰 JJ Lin

风到这里就是黏 黏住过客的思念
Fēng dào zhè lǐ jiù shì nián nián zhù guò kè de sī niàn
When the wind reaches here it turns sticky, sticking to the longing of passers-by
雨到了这里缠成线 缠着我们流连人世间
Yǔ dào le zhè lǐ chán chéng xiàn chán zhe wǒ men liú lián rén shì jiān
When the rain reaches here it twists into threads, entwining us lingering in the mortal world
你在身边就是缘 缘分写在三生石上面
Nǐ zài shēn biān jiù shì yuán yuán fèn xiě zài sān shēng shí shàng miàn
You by my side is fate, our destiny carved on the Three-Life Stone
爱有万分之一甜 宁愿我就葬在这一点
Ài yǒu wàn fēn zhī yī tián nìng yuàn wǒ jiù zàng zài zhè yī diǎn
Love is one ten-thousandth sweet, I’d rather be buried in that tiny bit
圈圈圆圆圈圈 天天年年天天的我 深深看你的脸
Quān quān yuán yuán quān quān tiān tiān nián nián tiān tiān de wǒ shēn shēn kàn nǐ de liǎn
Circles within circles, every day, every year, every day I gaze deeply at your face
生气的温柔 埋怨的温柔的脸
Shēng qì de wēn róu mán yuàn de wēn róu de liǎn
The gentle face when angry, the gentle face when blaming
不懂爱恨情仇煎熬的我们 都以为相爱就像风云的善变
Bù dǒng ài hèn qíng chóu jiān áo de wǒ men dōu yǐ wéi xiāng ài jiù xiàng fēng yún de shàn biàn
We who don’t understand love, hate, passion and torment all thought love was as fickle as the weather
相信爱一天 抵过永远 在这一刹那冻结了时间
Xiāng xìn ài yī tiān dǐ guò yǒng yuǎn zài zhè yī chà nà dòng jié le shí jiān
Believing one day of love outweighs eternity, in this instant time is frozen
不懂怎么表现温柔的我们 还以为殉情只是古老的传言
Bù dǒng zěn me biǎo xiàn wēn róu de wǒ men hái yǐ wéi xùn qíng zhǐ shì gǔ lǎo de chuán yán
We who don’t know how to show tenderness still thought dying for love was just an ancient legend
离愁能有多痛 痛有多浓 当梦被埋在江南烟雨中 心碎了才懂
Lí chóu néng yǒu duō tòng tòng yǒu duō nóng dāng mèng bèi mái zài jiāng nán yān yǔ zhōng xīn suì le cái dǒng
How painful can parting be, how intense the pain, only when dreams are buried in Jiangnan’s misty rain do we finally understand with a broken heart
